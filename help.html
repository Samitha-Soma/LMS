Help

Adding a Book

To add a book, click on the "Add Book" button. You will be prompted to enter the book's details, including the title, author, and genre.

Searching for a Book

To search for a book, enter the book's title or author in the search box and click on the "Search" button. The system will display a list of books that match your search criteria.

Returning a Book

To return a book, select the book from the list of borrowed books and click on the "Return" button. The system will update the book's status to "Available" and remove it from the list of borrowed books.

How to Use:

User Registration:

New users register by providing necessary details and a valid email address. A confirmation email is sent to verify the user's identity.
Browsing and Searching:

Users can browse the catalog or use the search feature to find books.
Filter options help narrow down search results.
Checking Out and Returning Books:

To borrow a book, users select the book and initiate the checkout process.
Books are returned through the user's account page.
Account Management:

Users can view their borrowing history, due dates, and update their account information.
Librarian Functions:

Librarians log in with administrative privileges.
They can add new books, manage user accounts, and view detailed reports.
Notifications:

Users receive notifications for overdue books or important announcements.
Librarians receive alerts for pending book requests or overdue books.
